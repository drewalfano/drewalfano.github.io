Canal District

Wayfinding, signage, and campaign work supporting the revitalization of the Canal District waterfront.

Hero image placeholder

The Challenge

The district needed a consistent visual language across signage, banners, and digital promotion to unify a stretch of waterfront still in the middle of redevelopment.

The Approach

A wayfinding system rooted in the canal's geometry, paired with a campaign identity built to work equally well on a lamppost banner and an Instagram story.
