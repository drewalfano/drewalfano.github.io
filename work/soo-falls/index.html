01 · Branding & Packaging

Soo Falls

Reviving Sault Ste. Marie's historic brewery through a bold, flexible identity and packaging system built for modern retail, hospitality, events, and local culture.

Role

Creative Lead & Sole Designer

Timeline

2024 — Present

Disciplines

Brand Identity, Packaging, Art Direction, Campaign Design, Social Media, Environmental Graphics, Outdoor Advertising, Print Collateral, Photography, Video, Web & Digital Design, Production Design

Selected Applications

8+ Beer Labels Seasonal Packaging LCBO Launch Materials Restaurant Partner Collateral Bus Advertising Zamboni Wraps Digital & Printed Billboards Table Toppers GFL Memorial Gardens Advertising Event Branding Social Media Campaigns Signage & History Walls

The Challenge

Soo Falls needed a brand that felt distinctly local without leaning on tired waterfall clichés, and a packaging system flexible enough to scale across a growing product line.

The Approach

A bold wordmark, a restrained color system, and a modular label grid let each product feel distinct while staying unmistakably part of the same family.

Project Scale

Eight-plus beer labels. LCBO launches. Bus wraps, billboards, and a Zamboni. One system built to hold all of it.
