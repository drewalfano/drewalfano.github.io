About

I'm Drew Alfano, a graphic designer and brand lead based in Sault Ste. Marie. I work across branding, packaging, campaigns, and digital design, building visual systems that stay consistent from a shelf label to a billboard to a scroll on a phone.

My process starts with structure: clear typography, a disciplined color system, and a grid that holds up whether it's applied to a single business card or a full retail environment.
