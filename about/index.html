About

I'm Drew Alfano, a graphic designer and brand lead based in Sault Ste. Marie. I build visual systems across packaging, campaigns, websites and physical applications, with a focus on making many moving parts feel like one clear brand.

My work has included reconstructing and modernizing established identities, developing packaging systems, planning websites, directing campaigns and preparing designs for production. I'm most interested in the point where strategy meets execution—creating a system that still feels consistent whether it appears on a can, a billboard or a phone.
